BARANGAY PROFILE

Cupang is dubbed the “Most Blessed Barangay in the City of Muntinlupa”. Through the development activities that have occurred from fishing and farming, the barangay now hosts some 50 major industries and 11 first class subdivisions. Land use is mainly industrial with sporadic residential zones. Cupang is also the home of prominent subdivisions in the city such as the Alabang Hills, Hillsborough, Intercity homes and many more. Cupang is bordered by Sucat to the north; Buli to the north-east, Parañaque City to the north-west; Alabang to the south; and the Laguna lake to the east.

HISTORY

In June 13, 1986, after the EDSA revolution, President Corazon C. Aquino appointed Attorney Ignacio Bunye as officer-in-charge of Muntinlupa City. This appointment is in lieu of nationwide revamp of local government units following the EDSA revolution in February of that year. Attorney Ignacio Bunye served the municipality for three consecutive terms together with Vice mayor Attorney Jaime R. Fresnedi. The administration implemented various projects that led to the municipality’s progression.
